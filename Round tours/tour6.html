15 days Sri Lanka Grand Odyssey

Home Round Tours 15 days Sri Lanka Grand Odyssey

"Sri Lanka Uncovered"

Embark on a captivating five-day Sri Lankan adventure. Begin in Colombo, the vibrant capital, exploring the National Museum and Gangarama Temple. Journey to Galle via Bentota, where you’ll visit the turtle hatchery and Galle Fort. Enjoy whale watching and beach relaxation in Mirissa. Discover Negombo’s bustling fish markets and historic Dutch Fort before your departure. This tour blends culture, nature, and history for an unforgettable experience.

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo (CMB) - Transfer to Negombo

Upon arrival at Colombo International Airport, you will be warmly welcomed by our English-speaking chauffeur and airport staff. After receiving assistance, you will be transferred to your hotel in Negombo (Approx. 30 min). Check-in and relax.

Day 2: Negombo - Pinnawala - Dambulla - Sigiriya

After breakfast, embark on a journey to Sigiriya. En route, visit the Dambulla Rock Cave Temple and either the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage or Millennium Elephant Foundation. In the afternoon, arrive in Sigiriya and check-in at your hotel.

Day 3: Sigiriya - Polonnaruwa - Sigiriya

After breakfast, explore the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Following the visit, proceed to the ancient city of Polonnaruwa (Approx. 1 hour) for a city tour, visiting the most famous historical sites. Return to your hotel in Sigiriya in the evening.

Day 4: Sigiriya - Kaudulla - Anuradhapura

Begin the day with an early morning jeep safari at Minneriya or Kaudulla National Park, where you can witness herds of elephants and other wildlife. In the afternoon, experience a village safari in Sigiriya to explore traditional lifestyles and cooking. Later, transfer to Anuradhapura and check-in at your hotel.

Day 5: Anuradhapura - Nilaveli - Trincomalee

Enjoy breakfast and embark on a sightseeing tour of Anuradhapura, including visits to Jaya Sri Maha Bodhi and Ruwanwelisaya Stupa. Then, proceed to Trincomalee, stopping at Nilaveli Beach (Approx. 2 hours). Check-in at your hotel and unwind by the beach.

Day 6: Trincomalee Exploration

After breakfast, explore Trincomalee with visits to the Kandaswamy Temple and scenic beach areas. Optional water activities such as scuba diving, snorkeling, or fishing are available. Spend the afternoon relaxing by the beach.

Day 7: Trincomalee - Pasikudah

After breakfast, travel to the serene Pasikudah Beach. Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and enjoy the afternoon at leisure.

Day 8: Pasikudah - Kandy

Following breakfast, proceed to Kandy. En route, visit the Matale Herbal Spice Garden (Approx. 4 hours 30 min). Upon arrival, check-in at your hotel and relax. In the evening, attend a vibrant Kandyan dance performance.

Day 9: Kandy - Nuwara Eliya

After breakfast, visit the Sacred Temple of the Tooth Relic (Dalada Maligawa), explore gem and batik shops, and then head to Nuwara Eliya. On the way, visit a tea factory and plantation. In the afternoon, visit Gregory Park and enjoy the cool climate.

Day 10: Nuwara Eliya - Ella - Yala

After breakfast, travel to Yala National Park (Approx. 4 hours). En route, visit the Nine Arch Bridge and Ella Rock viewpoint. In the afternoon, embark on a jeep safari at Yala National Park. Check-in at your hotel in Yala afterward.

Day 11: Yala - Mirissa

After breakfast, drive to Mirissa and check-in at your hotel. Spend the afternoon relaxing by the beach and enjoy Mirissa’s nightlife with a seafood dinner.

Day 12: Mirissa Exploration

Embark on an early morning whale-watching safari. Afterward, return to your hotel and spend the day at leisure, with optional water sports available.

Day 13: Mirissa - Galle

After breakfast, travel to Galle (Approx. 1 hour 15 min). In the afternoon, enjoy a city tour, visiting the Galle Fort Lighthouse and the National Museum. Check-in at your hotel and relax.

Day 14: Galle - Colombo

After breakfast, drive back to Colombo. Check-in at your hotel and enjoy a city tour, including Galle Face Beach, the National Museum, and Gangaramaya Temple. Engage in some shopping before relaxing at your hotel.

Day 15: Colombo - Departure

Following breakfast, transfer to the airport for your departure flight, carrying cherished memories of your Sri Lankan adventure.
