10 Days Sri Lanka Grand Discovery

Home Round Tours 10 Days Sri Lanka Grand Discovery

"The Island of Wonders"

Embark on a 10-day journey across Sri Lanka, exploring its rich culture, history, lush tea plantations, and stunning beaches. From ancient cities like Anuradhapura to the beauty of Sigiriya Rock and Kandy's Temple of the Tooth, enjoy scenic train rides, wildlife safaris in Yala, and relaxing beach days. Experience local cuisine, cultural shows, and more, with optional extensions to the Maldives or more beach time in Sri Lanka.

Day 1 : Welcome to Sri Lanka / Airport Pick-up

Ayubowan! Welcome to Sri Lanka, a land like no other. This tour covers ancient history, tea estates, wildlife, and beaches. Upon arrival at Colombo CMB airport, meet your tour leader. Transfer to Negombo, known for its beaches. Relax and enjoy the sunset. Overnight stay in a seafront hotel.

Day 2 : Royal Kingdom of Anuradhapura

Enjoy breakfast with Sri Lankan tea. Travel to Anuradhapura, a UNESCO site. Stop at Munneshwaram Temple en route. Explore Sri Maha Bodhi and Ruwanweliseya. Experience the ancient kingdom. Relax overnight in a nature-friendly resort.

Day 3: Ritigala Forest Monastery / Village Tour / Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Head to Dambulla after breakfast. Visit Ritigala Forest Monastery (weather permitting). Engage in a village tour with a Tuk Tuk ride and cooking demo. Visit Sigiriya Rock Fortress at sunset. Stay overnight in a nature-friendly resort in Dambulla.

Day 4 : Historic Kandy / Temple of Tooth Relic

Proceed to Kandy. Visit the Royal Cave Temple in Dambulla and Matale Spice Garden. Explore the Temple of the Tooth Relic and enjoy a cultural dance show. Stay overnight in a mountain-view hotel in Kandy.

Day 5: Hill Country Tea Plantations and Waterfalls

Travel to Nuwara Eliya, "Little England." Visit the Gem Museum and the Royal Botanical Garden. Drive through tea plantations and visit a tea factory. Walk through the city center and local market. Stay in a cozy villa in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 6 : Train Journey into the Mountains / Mini Adam's Peak

Take a scenic train ride from Nanu-Oya to Ella. Visit the Nine Arch Bridge and trek to Little Adam’s Peak. Relax in Ella village with restaurants and entertainment. Stay in a mountain-view hotel in Ella.

Day 7 : Yala National Park Jeep Safari

Travel to Yala National Park. Optionally, trek to Ella Rock for sunrise. Stop at Ravana waterfall. Enjoy a 4x4 jeep safari to spot leopards and elephants. Stay in a safari lodge in Tissamaharama.

Day 8 : Galle Fort / Turtle Hatchery / Beach Relax

Drive along the southern coast to Galle. Explore Galle Fort and visit the Galle Lighthouse. Visit a Turtle Hatchery Center. Relax and stay overnight in a beach resort in Hikkaduwa or Bentota.

Day 9 : Explore the South Coast / Beach Relax

Relax on the beach with white sands and palm trees. Enjoy a cocktail or beer. Walk along the seashore and indulge in authentic Sri Lankan seafood. Stay overnight in a beach resort in Hikkaduwa or Bentota.

Day 10 : Colombo City Tour / Airport or Hotel Drop-off

Visit Colombo’s vibrant streets and historical sites. Explore restaurants and modern shopping malls. Enjoy last-minute shopping before heading to the airport or your hotel for drop-off.
