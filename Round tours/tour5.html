12 Days of Tranquil Wonders

Home Round Tours 12 Days of Tranquil Wonders

"The Island of Wonders"

Embark on a captivating five-day Sri Lankan adventure. Begin in Colombo, the vibrant capital, exploring the National Museum and Gangarama Temple. Journey to Galle via Bentota, where you’ll visit the turtle hatchery and Galle Fort. Enjoy whale watching and beach relaxation in Mirissa. Discover Negombo’s bustling fish markets and historic Dutch Fort before your departure. This tour blends culture, nature, and history for an unforgettable experience.

Day 1: Airport or Hotel Pick-up / Welcome to Sri Lanka

Upon arrival at Colombo CMB Airport, meet your guide and transfer to a serene beachfront hotel in Negombo. Unwind and soak in the tropical vibes as you prepare for your adventure.

Day 2: Elephant Foundation / Village Tour / Sigiriya Fortress

Visit the Pinnawala Elephant Rehabilitation Centre, experience a cultural village tour with a Sri Lankan curry cooking demo, and conquer the iconic Sigiriya Lion Rock Fortress. Overnight stay at a nature-friendly resort in Sigiriya.

Day 3 : Kingdom of Polonnaruwa / Elephant Trail Jeep Safari

Cycle through the ancient ruins of Polonnaruwa and embark on a thrilling 4x4 Jeep Safari in Minneriya or Kaudulla National Park to spot wild elephants. Unwind with an optional Ayurvedic massage. Overnight stay in Sigiriya.

Day 4: Golden Cave Temple / Historic Kandy / Cultural Show

Explore the Dambulla Golden Cave Temple, stop by the Matale Spice Garden, and visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic in Kandy. Enjoy a traditional cultural dance show in the evening. Overnight stay with stunning mountain views in Kandy.

Day 5: Kitulgala Forest Adventures & Rafting

Experience the adrenaline rush of white water rafting in the Kelani River and trek to a natural rock pool with a 5-meter waterfall. Relax amidst the greenery. Overnight stay in Kandy or Kitulgala.

Day 6: Hill Country Tea Plantations and Waterfalls

Journey through the misty hills to Nuwara Eliya, visit a tea plantation, and enjoy a tea-tasting experience. Explore the Royal Botanical Garden and Nuwara Eliya’s charming colonial town. Overnight in a beautiful hilltop villa.

Day 7: Train Journey into the Mountains / Mini Adam's Peak

Hop on the world-famous train from Nanu-Oya to Ella, trek up Mini Adam’s Peak, and explore the Nine Arch Bridge and Ravana Waterfall. Overnight stay in a cozy mountain-view hotel.

Day 8: Yala National Park Leopard Trail Game Drive

Spot leopards, black bears, and elephants on a 4x4 Jeep Safari in Yala National Park. Optional early hike to Ella Rock for sunrise. Relax at a safari resort in Yala or Thissamaharama.

Day 9: Leisure Day on the Beach

Relax on the pristine beaches of Mirissa or Dickwella, swim in turquoise waters, and enjoy fresh seafood. Overnight at a beachfront hotel.

Day 10: Whale Watching / Galle Dutch Fort

Embark on an early morning whale-watching expedition, then explore the historic Galle Dutch Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Overnight stay in Galle.

Day 11: Bentota Beach / Turtle Hatchery

Enjoy water sports at Bentota Beach and visit a turtle hatchery. Take an optional boat safari in the Madu Ganga River. Overnight at a luxury beach resort.

Day 12: Colombo City Tour / Airport Transfer

Visit Colombo’s vibrant streets and historical sites. Explore restaurants and modern shopping malls. Enjoy last-minute shopping before heading to the airport or your hotel for drop-off.
