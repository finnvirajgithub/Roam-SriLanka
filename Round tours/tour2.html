"Cultural Gems of Sri Lanka"

This 4-day, 3-night tour offers an unforgettable experience of Sri Lanka's cultural and historical heritage. Visit the Elephant Orphanage in Pinnawala, the Temple of the Tooth in Kandy, the Botanical Garden in Peradeniya, and the ancient Sigiriya Rock. Explore Dambulla Cave Temple, the ruins of Polonnaruwa, and the ancient city of Anuradhapura. Enjoy scenic drives through lush landscapes, plantations, and historical sites, providing a deep dive into Sri Lanka's rich past and natural beauty.

Day 01 : Arrival – Colombo – Pinnawala – Kandy

Arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport and be greeted by Roam Sri Lanka Tours representatives. Travel from Colombo to Pinnawala to visit the famous elephant orphanage, where abandoned elephants are cared for. Witness baby elephants being bottle-fed and enjoy their river bath. Continue to Kandy, a historic royal city nestled between green hills. Visit the Temple of the Tooth Relic and experience a traditional Kandyan dance performance. Overnight stay in Kandy.

Day 02 : Kandy – Dambulla – Sigiriya

Start the day with a visit to the renowned Peradeniya Botanical Garden, known for its orchids and tropical flora. Journey north through scenic landscapes, including paddy fields and spice gardens. Visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site with intricate Buddha statues and Hindu deities. Spend the night in Sigiriya.

Day 03 : Sigiriya – Polonnaruwa – Sigiriya

Explore the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, a 200-meter-high monolith transformed into a fortress by King Kashyapa. Marvel at the ancient frescoes of the Sigiriya Maidens. In the afternoon, visit Polonnaruwa, the ancient capital of Sri Lanka from the 11th to the 13th century. Discover the well-preserved temples, dagobas, and royal palaces. Return to Sigiriya for an overnight stay.

Day 04 : Sigiriya – Anuradhapura – Departure

After breakfast, travel to Anuradhapura, the first capital of Sri Lanka. Explore ancient ruins, towering dagobas, temples, and the sacred Bo Tree. Journey back to Colombo, passing through lush paddy fields and coconut plantations. Arrive at the airport or your chosen destination in Colombo by evening.
